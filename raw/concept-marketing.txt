# TikTok Marketing Funnel for mojhit.pl

**Core Concept:** 
Use nano-influencers on TikTok and Instagram Reels to generate hype without upfront ad spend.

**Mechanics:**
1. We give influencers a promo code for 50 free coins (enough for 100 tracks).
2. They generate funny, weird, or highly relatable Polish songs (e.g., "song about eating pierogi at 3 AM" or "disco polo about my ex").
3. They post the video with the track and our hashtag #mojhitPL.
4. The link in their bio goes to our guest-generation page (Lead Magnet).

**Economics:**
- Target CPA (Cost Per Acquisition): < 2 PLN per registered user.
- The 50 coins cost us virtually nothing in API tokens but hold a perceived value of ~15 PLN.

**Milestone:** 
Launch this campaign immediately after the BLIK Stripe integration is verified.
