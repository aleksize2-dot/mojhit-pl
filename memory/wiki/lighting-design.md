# Lighting Design

## Overview
Lighting design (illumination) involves planning and designing lighting systems for architectural, stage/event, product, and urban environments.

## Key Principles
- **Three layers of light:** Ambient (general), Task (focused), Accent (highlighting)
- **Color temperature:** Warm (2700K-3000K) to cool (5000K+)
- **CRI (Color Rendering Index):** Measures light quality (80+ good, 90+ excellent)
- **Lumens vs Lux:** Lumens = total light output; Lux = illuminance at a surface

## Architectural Lighting
- **Project types:** Residential, commercial, public buildings, hospitality
- **Design process:** Concept → Schematic → Design development → Documentation → Implementation
- **Smart controls:** DALI, KNX, Zigbee, Wi‑Fi integration
- **Software tools:** Dialux, Relux, AutoCAD, SketchUp, visualization software

## Resources
- **Standards:** IES (Illuminating Engineering Society), CIBSE (Chartered Institution of Building Services Engineers)
- **Community:** Lighting Analysts, LightShow West/East
