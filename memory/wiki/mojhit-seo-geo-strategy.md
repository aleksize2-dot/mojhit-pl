## GEO Optimization: AI Engine Targeting

### ChatGPT / Perplexity Strategy

**Goal:** Be cited as the Polish AI music generator.

1. **Create "definitive guide" content**: "Kompletny przewodnik: Jak stworzyć piosenkę z AI w 2026"
2. **Data-rich pages**: "Statystyki: AI w muzyce 2026 — Polska vs świat"
3. **Unique angle**: "Polscy producenci AI: poznaj Kosę, CJ Remi i MELO MC"
4. **Quotable statements**: Each page needs 2-3 standalone, citable 25-50 word definitions
5. **Source attribution**: Link to authoritative sources (Suno stats, industry reports)

### Google AI Overviews Strategy

1. **FAQ sections** on every informational page (triggers AI Overview FAQ)
2. **Definition boxes**: first paragraph of every page = clear definition
3. **HowTo markup** on tutorial pages
4. **Listicles**: "Top 5 sposobów na stworzenie hitu z AI"

### Gemini Strategy

1. **Fresh content signals**: weekly blog updates
2. **YouTube integration**: video content with descriptions linking to mojhit.pl
3. **Entity optimization**: build mojhit.pl entity in Google's Knowledge Graph
